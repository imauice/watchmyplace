# WatchMyPlace

## Mission

Help people stay aware of risks around the places they care about.

Users should not need to constantly monitor weather, water levels, news, or sensors.

They simply pin a place.

We watch it.

---

# Tagline

# Core Philosophy

Collect Facts

↓

Discover Patterns

↓

Predict Impacts

---

# Place First

People do not care about events.

People care about places.

Examples:

🏠 Home

🏫 School

🏢 Office

🏥 Hospital

🏪 Shop

WatchMyPlace always starts from places.

---

# Observation First

Everything is an Observation.

Examples:

- Weather forecast
- Rain
- River level
- PM2.5
- Community report
- Volunteer report
- Sensor data
- Government announcement
- Historical record

All observations share one common model.

---

# Intelligence

The system should NOT start with AI.

The system should first become good at collecting facts.

Over time:

Observations

↓

Repeated Patterns

↓

Discovered Relations

↓

Living Risk Knowledge

↓

Impact Prediction

---

# Community

Everyone can help.

Users report only what they see.

No expertise required.

Small observations from many people create a better understanding of the world.

---

# Notification Philosophy

Silence is valuable.

The application should stay quiet most of the time.

Notify only when:

- the watched place may be affected
- there is enough confidence
- the user still has time to act

---

# Product Principles

1. Place First
2. Observation First
3. Community Powered
4. Explainable Decisions
5. Modular Workers
6. Simple before Smart
7. Data before AI

---

# Long-term Vision

Build an observation-driven risk intelligence platform.

Continuously learn from:

- Public data
- Sensors
- Community observations
- Historical outcomes

Discover patterns naturally.

Predict impacts earlier.

Help communities become more resilient.

WatchMyPlace collects real-world observations, discovers meaningful patterns, and predicts impacts for the places people care about.
